About Us

We are the subset of the beneficiaries of our great earth “Urvi”, with a passion to give back some good to the society and nature, thus to our next generation. Our motto is “Give what you would like to receive”

We are committed to:
  • Produce natural and chemical-free products
  • Recycle and Reuse
  • Work close to nature, ancient science, and our culture
Our range of products We are very picky and choosy when it comes to the range of products. We produce or promote only if we are 100% convinced that the product is 100% nature friendly and committed to continuous improvisations.
